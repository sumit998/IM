About Us

We feel pleasure to introduce ourselves as Innovative Machineries Agro India Pvt Ltd one of the startup company based in Greater Noida.

We deal in Agro Machineries currently engaged in manufacturing of Pneumatic based Rice Threshing Machine. The company was founded in 2016 and incorporated as PVT LTD in 2017. The punchline of our company is "Thinking Solution For Farmers". With our patented machinery named Apna Baju-A PneumaticRice Based Machine,we deliever cutting edge and rugged solutions for rural areas.

Our machine has been well recognized by GES-2017, Startup Accelerator India,Govin capital,Kalam Centre-AKTU,DST and MSME,Govt of India for innovative idea used in it.

With satisfactory performance, high quality product delivery and precise exhibtion of service,it will sustain itself according to the need of the farmers in National and International Market
